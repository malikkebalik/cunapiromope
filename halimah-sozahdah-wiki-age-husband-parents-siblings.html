    Secrets and Sisterhood Star Halimah Sozahdah

    Halimah Sozahdah (age: 46 years; born on June 1, 1977) is a County Ombudsman in California. According to People, Halimah is an activist and runs various education programs.

    She is widely popular as the sister of Shakur Sozahdah, a famous Afghan-American model who made waves in the early 2000s.

    In June 2023, Hali made her place in the entertainment industry with her appearance in the reality show, “Secrets & Sisterhood: The Sozahdahs,” which aired on June 7 on different OTT platforms like Disney+, Hulu, and Star+.

    The show presents a close look at the lifestyle of the Sozahdah sisters. Gwyn Frank has done a fantastic job covering the professional, personal, and family life of Halimah and her 9 siblings.

    When was Halimah Sozahdah Born?

    On June 1, 1977, Halimah Sozahdah (age 46) was born in Kabul, Afghanistan. She is the 2nd child in her family with 9 sisters: Shaista, Muzlefa, Nooreya, Rabya, Shakur, Jamila, Khadija, Siddiqa, and Hamida.

    Hali was a toddler when she and her parents had to leave Afghanistan due to political unrest. Fortunately, Halimah’s dad decided to come to the United States and settle in California.

    In Riverside, CA, this Sozahdah sister grew up attending a school named John W. North High School. She was a brilliant student, and that was reflected in her grades. In 1995, she completed high school and enrolled at a reputed university for further studies.

    Is Halimah Sozahdah Married?

    According to records, Halimah was married to a person named Mustafa, but the wedding lasted only a few years. In 2006 the couple filed for divorce and got separated.

    After Mustafa, a man named Albert Lobo came into Halimah’s life. Lobo is a Pakistani immigrant who came to the U.S. due to religious intolerance in Pakistan. He worked as a detective in San Bernadino until he lost his legs and arms to pneumonia.

    According to The Press-Enterprise, Lobo caught an infection while working at Victorville jail. Despite the adverse situations, Lobo did not lose courage and became a source of motivation for others.

    In tough times Halimah, Lobo’s then-fiancee, stood by his side and cared for him. In many news articles, Sozahdah was portrayed as a loving partner who did not leave her partner in crisis, although it seems like Albert and Halimah are not together anymore.

    Halimah Sozahdah’s Profession

    Halimah belongs to a high-achieving family. All of her sisters are leading successful professional lives; Hamida is a PA, Muzlefa is a financial advisor, Shaista is a restaurateur, and Shakur is a model.
