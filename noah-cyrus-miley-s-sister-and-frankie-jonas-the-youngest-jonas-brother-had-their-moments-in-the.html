    Miley Cyrus and the Jonas Brothers starred in a few different television shows for Disney’s television network. The younger siblings of these stars, however, had their own moment with the Walt Disney Company. Here’s what we know about Noah Cyrus, Frankie Jonas, and the Walt Disney Studios animated movie, Ponyo.

    Miley Cyrus and the Jonas Brothers appeared on multiple Disney Channel television shows

    Some know her as the troublemaking and adorable Miley Stewart, who often performs as her alter-ego Hannah Montana. Thanks to Disney Channel, this character even had cameos in Disney Channel original series’ like The Suite Life of Zack and Cody and Suite Life on Deck.

    Miley Cyrus appeared in a few movies created by Disney, including The Hannah Montana Movie, Hannah Montana and Miley Cyrus: Best of Both Worlds Concert, and The Last Song. Since then, however, Miley Cyrus went on to star in the Netflix original series, Black Mirror, and even create her own Instagram series, Bright Minded.

    First enjoying a cameo on the Disney Channel original series, Hannah Montana, the Jonas Brothers enjoyed their own television shows, both Jonas and its spinoff series, Jonas LA. Since their time with Disney, these performers branched off for their solo projects and eventually came back together as the Jonas Brothers. 

    Both Miley Cyrus and the Jonas Brothers have younger siblings 

    Miley Cyrus isn’t the only star in her family, and we’re not just talking about the “Old Town Road” performer, Billy Ray Cyrus. Miley Cyrus’ younger sister, Noah, has since gone on to release her own music and star in a few movies. 

    Kevin, Joe, and Nick have one younger brother, named Frankie, who has since graduated from college (Belmont University.) On social media, this younger brother appears alongside the other Jonas performers, spending time with Kevin Jonas’ children. Although, when Noah Cyrus and Frankie Jonas were younger, they also enjoyed starring roles on Disney’s television network.

    Noah Cyrus and Frankie Jonas actually appeared in a few Disney Channel projects 

    These two had cameos on both of their siblings’ television shows. For one episode of Hannah Montana, Miley Stewart had to climb a rope for gym class, although she was super afraid to get to the top. Noah Cyrus appeared at the top of the rope already, beating her older sister. 

    Frankie Jonas had a pretty large role in the Jonas Brothers’ television series Jonas L.A. and Jonas. He appeared as the trio’s younger brother, even being called by his first name. However, these two starred in the English version of one “Walt Disney Studios” animated movie, released during 2008.

    Ponyo told the story of a goldfish princess who encounters a human boy named Sosuke. Although Noah Cyrus and Frankie Jonas only voice these characters for the English version, (in addition to Tina Fey, Matt Damon, and Cate Blanchett,) they ended up singing a song for Ponyo, as well.
