    Anne Hathaway’s baby boy has arrived. In 2015, it was confirmed that the actress was pregnant with baby number one. Ann, alongside her husband Adam Shulman, has reportedly been trying to get pregnant for quite a while. Now, we can confirm that Anne gave birth to a healthy baby boy – last month!

    Anne Hathaway and Adam Shulman are relatively private when it comes to their marriage and personal life. The actress didn’t share too much with the public about her pregnancy. So, it makes sense that she and Adam kept the birth of their newborn baby boy quiet for as long as possible.

    According to a report from E! Online, Anne Hathaway and her husband’s baby was born on March 24 in Los Angeles. The actress’s rep confirmed that the new addition to their family had arrived. According to E! Online, the proud parents named the healthy baby boy Jonathan Rosebanks Shulman. An insider close to the family dished, “The baby is extremely healthy and is surrounded by friends and family in L.A. Anne and Adam are ecstatic to be parents.”

    So, when will we see pictures of little Jonathan Rosebanks Shulman? Well, don’t hold your breath – the couple will most likely keep their baby boy out of the public eye for as long as possible. Anne Hathaway actually expressed concerns in interviews before she was even pregnant about the paparazzi and how it would affect her future children. The last thing Anne wanted was her future babies’ photos plastered all over the internet and her children flocked by the paparazzi whenever they left the house. If we do get to see photos of the happy new family – it will most likely be quite a while from now.
