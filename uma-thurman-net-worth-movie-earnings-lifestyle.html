    Famous for her ethereal looks, Uma Thurman, an American actress with diverse filmography, has a net worth of an enormous $45 million as of December 2023.

    Movie Earnings

    Uma has starred in tons of movies over the years and has earned a decent sum to fulfill her every desire.

    For her role in the 1992 film ‘Final Analysis’, she was paid $200,000 and also for ‘Jennifer Eight’.
