    Ryan Edwards Wrote Another Post Claiming Mackenzie Was Unfaithful

    He’s back at it!

    Ryan Edwards took to social media to accuse Mackenzie Standifer of an affair…again.

    Somehow, this post is even worse than the allegations that he wrote online only last week.

    This time, the Teen Mom star shared a suggestive image on Instagram of the mother of three posing in a leather jacket with what appears to be nothing underneath.

    The photo was shocking in itself, but Ryan wrote a dramatic caption that made serious claims about his wife being unfaithful.

    “If you guys have never seen a spineless sl*t this is one,” wrote the 35-year-old. “They take your money sleep with anything that looks at them.”

    The next part made less sense. Ryan added, “But they must have a wife to.”

    He concluded, “God what a joke. It’s sad really”

    The comments were filled with horrified users chiming in. Many alluded to Ryan’s past struggles with substance abuse.

    Someone advised, “Time to call your sponsor, man. Your kids don’t need this happening on social media.”

    Other people believed that this photo looked like it was taken by Mackenzie for her husband. They pointed out that she is wearing her wedding ring in the snap and it appears to be in some kind of book that may be intended as a gift.

    “For all we know this was taken for you, a snapshot doesn’t prove anything,” read another comment. “Please consider the impact of this hurting others hurts you.”

    This is only the latest wild claim from Ryan about the mother of his youngest children.

    Last week, Ryan went on Mackenzie’s page and commented on an old photo of the couple, “Take wife down off this I’m not proud of being married to someone that stays out in bars and goes home with other guys…”

    “Tomorrow can’t get here fast enough!” Ryan continued. “Don’t you know what happens when u lay with dogs?….wait look at who I’m talking to. And then blaming it on my addiction.”
