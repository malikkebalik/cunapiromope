    Nicki Minaj is known for her incredible talent as a hip-hop artist, and she’s done a ton with her career in a short amount of time. From “Anaconda” to “Starships,” there’s at least a song or two every radio listener recognizes. So, what is Nicki Minaj’s age? And how old is her young son? Here’s what we know about her personal life.

    What is Nicki Minaj’s age?

    When it comes to making it big, it wasn’t easy for Minaj to get ahead. Biography notes she was initially born in Saint James, Trinidad and Tobago, and moved to New York when she was 5 years old. She also went through a number of personas before settling on “Nicki Minaj,” too. Her birth name is Onika Tanya Maraj, and when she was young, she went by names that ranged from “Cookie” to “Harajuku Barbie” before settling on what we all know her as today.

    By age 12, Minaj knew she wanted to get involved with music and acting. And she later became a backup singer for New York City rappers before making it big with her own material. Eventually, it was her connection to Lil Wayne that helped launch her to stardom. The two started collaborating on mixtapes back in 2007. By 2010, Minaj put out her first album.

    Since then, the rest’s been history for Minaj. Her 2012 Super Bowl performance helped launch her to even more success. And she later started branching out into the world of acting. In 2019, Minaj started the Nicki Wrld Tour in Frankfurt, Germany.

    So, what is Nicki Minaj’s age? She was born on Dec. 8, 1982, making her 38 years old. She’ll turn 39 in 2021.

    How old is Nicki Minaj’s son?

    Minaj isn’t in the music spotlight as much as she once was. And that may be partially due to her family situation. Minaj has a young son with her husband, Kenneth Petty.

    Back in January 2021, she posted photos of her little boy and added a sweet caption. “Thank you for your love & support throughout this journey,” she wrote. “It’s meant so much to me. Becoming a mom is by far the most fulfilling job I’ve ever taken on. Sending love to all the superhero mothers out there. Big hugs to all the women who have been pregnant during this challenging time.”

    Minaj is quite private with her son, too. She calls him “Papa Bear” on Instagram but doesn’t reference him by name otherwise. However, she did reveal she almost named him “Ninja.”

    “You know what his name was supposed to be? I think l might still change it,” she said, according to Entertainment Tonight. “A lot of people said they liked it, but I said, ‘Nah.’ His name was going to be Ninja.”

    As for how old her son is, he was born on Sept. 30, 2020, making him around 4 months old.

    Nicki Minaj and husband Kenneth Petty knew each other as teens

    Minaj has been with her husband, Kenneth Petty, since December 2018, Us Weekly reports. Unfortunately, Minaj received a ton of backlash for her boyfriend because he was convicted of first-degree rape in 1995. But she didn’t let that stop her, as the couple had a longer history than most knew.

    “Nicki and Kenneth dated when she was a teenager, before she was famous,” a source claimed. “They linked up when she handed out turkeys for Thanksgiving in her hometown.”

    By early 2019, the two were already sparking marriage rumors. And they reportedly married in October 2019, according to Minaj’s Instagram. Today, they’re still going strong.

    As for Minaj’s age when she met Petty, it’s tough to say. They were technically just kids when they first knew each other, but their romance sparked when Minaj was 36.
