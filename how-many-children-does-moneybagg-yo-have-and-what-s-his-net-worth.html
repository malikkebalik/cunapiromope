    Moneybagg Yo makes headlines for his music and personal life. The Memphis native dropped his Time Served project in January and it peaked on the Billboard charts at number 3. Riding the wave, the rapper released a deluxe version in May which featured seven new tracks.

    Signed to Yo Gotti’s label in 2016, Moneybagg Yo stays on his hustle in order to earn money and success. He wants to take care of himself and his loved ones.

    His current girlfriend stays busy on social media, and sometimes gets into feuds with his children’s mothers, but he talks about fatherhood all the time. Learn more about Moneybagg Yo’s fatherly duties and his net worth.

    Moneybagg Yo is dating Ari Fletcher

    For a while, the rapper was dating Megan Thee Stallion, but the two broke up in 2019. Moneybagg Yo then moved on with Ari Fletcher, and the pair recently celebrated her birthday the weekend of July 11.

    They set the internet on fire with photos and videos from Fletcher’s party, which featured luxurious décor and a blinged-out cake.

    Fans were stunned when Moneybagg Yo gifted her with a new Lamborghini Urus SUV. The celebration and generous gesture made him a trending topic and attracted the attention of at least one of his children’s mothers, who went on a rant.

    It’s nothing new. Sometimes, Fletcher exchanges words with Moneybagg Yo’s kids’ mothers, but how many children are involved?

    Moneybagg Yo has multiple children

    The last time Moneybagg gave a head count for his brood was during a 2017 interview on Shade 45.

    Back then, he shared he had seven children, and he was on good terms with all their mothers. Since then, he’s had at least one more child—a baby girl named Spring—who’s been seen with him in social media videos as recently as April.

    Moneybagg Yo said his family was his inspiration and told the radio show hosts that when he dropped out of high school in 12th grade, he already had two or three children.

    He felt that the path he was heading down wouldn’t cut it and became serious about music. The soon-to-be 29-year-old successfully carved out a career for himself and has plans to grow his own empire.

    Moneybagg Yo has plans for his music and investments

    According to his interview with Revolt, the rapper intends to release more new music before 2020 ends. Additionally, Moneybagg dropped an apparel line called Bread Gang and wants to expand the line with new merchandise.

    Besides music and clothing, he’s set to appear in a feature film called 24 Hours, but the “U Played” artist is switching some things up. He’s known for spending money on jewelry but decided to put such purchases on pause to invest in real estate.

    He spoke with REAL 92.3 and said he’s building a business with Yo Gotti’s CMG and N-Less Entertainment. Future plans include land and property deals. Moneybagg Yo’s current net worth is estimated to be between $1-$1.5 million, per Celebrity Net Worth.
