    Biography:

    Emily Alyn Lind was born on May 6, 2002 in Brooklyn, New York. Her parents are producer John Lind and One Tree Hill actress Barbara Alyn Woods. Her sisters, Natalie and Alyvia, are also actresses. When she was around six, she debuted as young Lily Owens in the 2008 film The Secret Life of Bees. She next had a role in the movie Enter the Void. On television, she first played recurring role Emily Gardener in Eastwick.

    In 2010, she played Emma Lavery in the soap opera All My Children. She then became known for playing the young Amanda Clarke in the ABC series Revenge. In 2011, she portrayed Shirley Temple in the movie J. Edgar. She later had performances in the films Won’t Back Down, The Haunting in Connecticut 2: Ghosts of Georgia, Jackie & Ryan, and Mockingbird. She went on to star in the films Lights Out, The Babysitter, and Replicas. Her latest film role was in 2019’s Doctor Sleep. She also played Ariel in the TV series Code Black.
