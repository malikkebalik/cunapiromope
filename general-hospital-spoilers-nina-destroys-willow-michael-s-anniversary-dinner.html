    General Hospital (GH) spoilers reveal that Nina Corinthos (Cynthia Watros) will catch a glimpse of something that puts a smile on her face. It turns out Michael Corinthos (Chad Duell) will bring Willow Corinthos (Katelyn MacMullen) to the Metro Court for a very special occasion, so Nina will see them all dressed up from across the room.

    GH’s weekly preview video shows Michael taking Willow’s hand and wishing her a happy anniversary as she smiles.

    There’ll be some sweet moments during Willow and Michael’s celebratory night out, but Nina could be tempted to derail it.

    Nina has certainly had a hard time staying away from Willow in the past.

    No matter how many times Willow tries to convince Nina that their mother-daughter bond is shattered forever, Nina doesn’t seem willing to give up.

    With that in mind, Nina might decide to send a special dessert or bottle of bubbly to Willow and Michael’s table.

    Perhaps Nina could think of it as a peace offering and hope that it’s viewed as a kind gesture.

    Of course, it may be wishful thinking on Nina’s part if she actually believes Michael and Willow will respond well to any gifts from her!

    That’s especially true if Nina decides to deliver this peace offering to Willow and Michael’s table herself.

    It could lead to Willow getting agitated all over again and pushing Nina to back off.

    However, Michael and Willow are probably the ones who need to steer clear of the Metro Court considering Nina owns half of it.

    If Willow and Michael want to avoid tense Nina encounters, it might be best to pick another restaurant. Michael and Willow will be asking for trouble by showing up at Nina’s place of business.

    Nevertheless, there’s a chance that things may turn out OK after all. It’s possible that Nina will learn to restrain herself for once and keep her distance.

    On the other hand, maybe Willow and Michael will find a way to civil to Nina if she approaches them.

    We’re seeing sudden changes in characters’ behavior lately now that the new writing team is in charge.

    Perhaps Michael and Willow could have a slightly more pleasant attitude toward Nina than before.

    Our predictions point to some surprises in the works for Nina, so we’ll offer updates on all the stunning news ahead.

    Whatever the case, General Hospital spoilers say Michael and Willow will be walking into risky territory, so stay tuned to see how their dinner will go during the week of April 15-19!
