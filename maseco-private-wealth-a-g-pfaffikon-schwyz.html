    Maseco Private Wealth A.G. is a large advisory firm based in Pfaffikon Schwyz. It manages $388.79 million of regulatory assets for 99 client accounts. It has been registered with the SEC as an adviser since 2013 and has operated in the jurisdictions of California, Florida, Illinois, and 4 other states.

    Maseco Private Wealth A.G provides portfolio management for individuals and small businesses and portfolio management for businesses and institutional clients. It doesn't provide financial planning services to its clients. On top of advisory services, the firm doesn't engage in other business activities.

    Maseco Private Wealth A.G: assets under management

    The assets under management (AUM) of Maseco Private Wealth A.G. is $388.79 million. The firm's AUM has grown by 14% since the beginning of its operation, and the company doesn't manage any assets belonging to foreign clients.


    The firm manages $238.13 million in discretionary accounts and $150.66 million in non-discretionary accounts. On average, the discretionary accounts where all decisions are made on behalf of the client hold $3.78 million each. Non-discretionary accounts, where clients actively manage their portfolios, generally hold around $4.19 million.

    Fees & Commissions

    The advisory firm has a single stream of revenue. Clients pay Maseco Private Wealth A.G. for their services and products based on a percentage of assets under management.
