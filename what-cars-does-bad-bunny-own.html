    BMW M4

    (Source: Instagram)

    Reportedly the first car Ocasio purchased after making a name for himself as a musician was a white BMW M4. The M4 is the weapon of choice for gearheads thanks to its thrilling driving dynamics and excellent performance at higher speeds. Ocasio’s M4 is from the first generation of the series and features a two-door coupe which is an upgrade from its predecessor, the M3.

    The car features a 3L Twin-turbocharged inline-six petrol engine which is capable of producing 424 bhp and a maximum torque of over 550 nm. The car comes with a 7-speed dual-clutch transmission which is the same one the M3 used, with slightly improved mechanics. With these upgrades, BMW claims the M4 can go from 0-60 in 3.8 seconds and has a top speed of 180 mph.

    As of 2022, this car is worth $70,000.

    Mercedes G-Wagon

    (Source: Instagram)

    An icon of pop culture, the Mercedes-Benz G-Class is not only a popular SUV among rappers but also among other prominent celebrities. The G-Wagon that is owned by Bad Bunny is one of the most powerful renditions of this SUV over the years.

    The black Mercedes Benz G63 AMG features an all-black paint job that extends to the full body of the car and is finished off with a chrome-finished bull bar ahead of the grill of the car. The car comes with a 5.5L bi-turbo V8 petrol engine that is capable of producing over 536 bhp and a peak torque of 739 nm. A signature all-wheel drive, the G63 AMG features a seven-speed automatic gearbox that allows it to channel all its power evenly across the 4x4.

    Rolls Royce Dawn

    (Source: Bad Bunny YouTube)

    One of the most expensive cars in his collection, Bad Bunny’s Rolls Royce Dawn is reportedly worth $368,850. The car is even featured in the music video for his popular song, ’Yo Perreo Sola’. Ocasio’s Dawn features a customized pink color interior scheme for the seats and upholstery for doors and dashboard, and has subtle white color on the outside.

    Based on the Ghost, the Dawn is essentially a two-door convertible version of the Ghost and features a 6.6L twin-turbo V12 petrol engine. It is capable of producing over 562 bhp and a maximum torque of 820 Nm. The value of this car will perpetually increase as the Dawn is no longer in production.

    Bugatti Chiron

    (Source: Instagram)

    One of the most exquisite cars in the world, the Bugatti Chiron also happens to be one of the most powerful cars ever produced by French hypercar specialist Bugatti. A replacement to the mighty Veyron, the Chiron features a body made out of carbon fiber and comes with an 8L quad-turbocharged W16 petrol engine which can churn out a staggering 1478 bhp and is capable of reaching a maximum torque of 1,600 Nm.

    Currently, the Bugatti Chiron retails at a base price of over $3 million.

    Toyota Corolla

    (Source: Instagram)

    Let’s be honest, this is not a car you would expect to be owned by a globally recognized musician with an estimated net worth of $18 million. You may think this was Ocasio’s first car before he rose to fame but in reality, Ocasio purchased this Corolla as a replacement for his Bugatti Chiron. Yes, he replaced a $3 million car with a $20,525 generic Toyota. This classic 2003 model features a 1.8L petrol engine that is paired with a CVT gearbox and produces a maximum of 132 bhp and a peak torque of 172 Nm.

    The reason why Ocasio purchased this old second-hand Corolla was because of all the unwanted attention his Bugatti Chiron would earn him in his native country of Puerto Rico. This problem persisted while he was in America as well and this forced him to get rid of his Chiron and get a basic Toyota so he could live his life undisturbed.
