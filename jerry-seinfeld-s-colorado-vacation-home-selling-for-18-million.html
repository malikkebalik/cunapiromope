    Jerry Seinfeld, the richest and likely funniest comedian ever, is selling his Colorado vacation home for a whopping $18 million. Just one of many multi-million dollar properties bought thanks to his show about "nothing". This house is a sprawling mountainside lodge, located in Telluride, Colorado. It's a popular spot for the rich and famous, with the average house value in his ZIP code between $2.5 and $3 million. He'll catch neighbors Ralph Lauren, Tom Cruise and Katie Holmes when hitting the ski slopes of Telluride.

    Related Post: Jerry Seinfeld Net Worth

    For $18 million, you'll be buying a massive 27 acre lot of pristine mountainside woodlands with a massive wooden house to match totalling 14,200 square feet. To complete this massive property Seinfeld actually bought the two surrounding lots in 2007, one for $7.5 million and the other for $2.3 million. Plus he also completed a series of million dollar expansions and renovations. And the best part of the deal? All of Jerry's furnishings are included! So this is great buy for any obsessed Seinfeld fans with $18 million to blow on memorabilia.

    Once you climb the private driveway and manicured gardens you'll find Seinfeld's eco-friendly home. The house was built entirely out of wood and stone with 11 bedrooms, 11 full bathrooms and 3 half bathrooms. Inside you'll find hardwood floors, wood walls, wood-beamed ceilings and huge windows. The double-height living room has a huge stone fireplace to match. The expansive kitchen and dining area is perfect for his wife Jessica who is a best-selling cookbook author. It also includes several breakfast/snack bars, a great match for Jerry who is a well known breakfast cereal fanatic. The home has two mini-libraries (books included), two lofts, hut tubs, steam room, multiple fireplaces and a wet bar. He has an epic entertainment patio at 5,500 square feet that includes views of the mountain peak used in the Coors Brewery ads. There is also a heated 4 car garage and a guesthouse hidden on the estate.

    Another big celebrity vacation home in Colorado is also selling for $7.9 million. It used to be the property of fellow prime time comedian Kelsey Grammer, but is now being sold by Ex-wife and current Real Housewives of Beverly Hills star Camille Grammer.
